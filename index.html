Welcome to OPIDF

The Open Platform Irish Dancing Federation (OPIDF) was established in 2018 by a group of innovative and forward-thinking professional dance teachers. Our primary aim is to promote and advance the teaching of Irish dancing, providing dancers with a platform to showcase their talents in a fun, fair and friendly environment.

We host Grade, Championship, National, and World Championship Irish dancing events that teachers, dancers, and families love. OPIDF events are more than just a weekend of dancing and competing — they are memorable, rewarding and positive experiences for all involved.
