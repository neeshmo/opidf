About OPIDF

Open Platform Irish Dancing Federation (OPIDF) set out clear rules and guidelines which allow our federation to flourish. Established in 2018 by a group of innovative and forward thinking professional dance teachers, their primary aim was to promote and advance the teaching of Irish dancing and provide dancers with a platform to showcase their talents in a fun, fair and friendly environment.

As a federation we host Grade, Championship, National and World Championship Irish dancing events that teachers, dancers and their families love.

OPIDF events are more than just a weekend of dancing and competing. Our federation members and adjudicators work hard to guarantee everyone who attends OPIDF events has a truly memorable, rewarding and positive experience.
